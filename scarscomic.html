About The Scars Project

This was the project that I created towards the end of my internship at Swiss Institute. I presented my project on the last day of the internship during an exhibition. The story that is shown here is only the prologue. I have been developing the Scars story for a while now, so there is definitely much more to tell in this universe! I wish I had been able to show more of the story, but I unfortunately only had one week to complete this specific project. I am definitely still working on developing the story further and hopefully, I'll be able to share more of it!
